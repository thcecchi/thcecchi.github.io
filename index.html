Runner

Runner is a social betting application built as my final project for the Iron Yard Academy. The application allows users to place bets against other users on the outcomes of daily NBA games. The results of the bets are then recorded in the app's main feed. ** This project is no longer maintained **

Spout

Spout is a social search application allowing users to enter their location, search for bars close to them and see a rating, category, and reviews for each establishment. Ratings and category are calculated based on the majority concenus of all reviews.

Front-end design and development

Hey! I'm Tyler

I'm a former ad agency account guy who traded in slide decks for a text editor and went to build a career developing great digital products and experiences. Great experiences have the power to invoke emotion, action and evangalism. They have the power to bring brands to life and become a part of people's lives.

Though building digital products is my craft, I approach the design and development process with both users and businesses in mind. Products are at their best when they serve both emotional and commerical purpose which is why I am committed to building solutions that expose a win-win scenario that achieves both.

Learning to build great products of any kind is a life-long endevour and my insatiable appetite for learning and creating constantly pushes me to design and build more beautiful interfaces, more powerful interactions, and more sophisticated products.
