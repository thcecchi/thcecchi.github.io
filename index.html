Work

Runner

Runner is a social betting application built as my final project for the Iron Yard Academy. The application allows users to place bets against other users on the outcomes of daily NBA games. The results of the bets are then recorded in the app's main feed to be commented on and viewed by all.

UI/UX design, Full-stack development

Spout

Spout is a social search application allowing users to enter their location, search for bars close to them and see a rating, category, and reviews for each establishment. Ratings and category are calculated based on the majority concenus of all reviews.

UI/UX design, Front-end development
